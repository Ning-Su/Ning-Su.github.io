Boston Weather 2017

I made the line chart to visualize the Boston weather in 2017. The 12 buttons can help people to check each month of temperature in 2017. Each chart has two lines. Red line represents the maximum temperature and blue line represents the minimum temperature. X axis represents the day of each month and y axis represents the Fahrenheit degree of temperature.

As we can see, January is the coldest month in Boston 2017, The temperature rose slightly in February, but later the overall average temperature in March was a bit colder than in February. The temperature in the next few months has been rising, until August, the highest temperature can reach 98 degrees. So, August is the hottest month in 2017. Then, the temperature gradually decreases after August.
